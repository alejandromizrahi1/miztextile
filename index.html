Who We Are

A Bridge Between China's Best Mills and North American Manufacturers

MizTextile LLC was founded with a singular mission: to make world-class textile raw materials accessible to manufacturers in Mexico and the United States. We partner directly with China's most reliable yarn mills to ensure consistent quality, competitive pricing, and dependable delivery.

Our clients are fabric manufacturers who supply some of the most demanding commercial sectors — from the vibrant floors of casino gaming rooms to the reverent aisles of houses of worship. Every spool we deliver carries the weight of that final product.
