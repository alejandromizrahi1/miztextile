Boucle Yarn

Boucle's signature looped, knotted surface creates an instantly recognizable textured fabric beloved in luxury upholstery and decorative applications. Its irregular loops add dimensional visual depth, making it a designer favorite for hospitality and hotel environments.

  • Structure Multi-ply with looped effect yarn
  • Fiber Polyester / Nylon blends
  • Texture Fine loops to coarse boucle
  • Applications Luxury Upholstery, Decorative Fabric, Wall Covering
  • MOQ Available on request

Air-Textured

Semi Tazlan Yarn

Semi Taslan is an air-textured yarn that combines a matte, wool-like aesthetic with the durability of synthetic fiber. Its bulky, slightly fleecy surface creates rich, tactile fabrics used in premium carpets, rugs, and contract upholstery across hospitality and worship spaces.

  • Process Air-jet texturing (Taslan process)
  • Fiber Polyester / Nylon
  • Finish Semi-matte, wool-like appearance
  • Applications Premium Carpets, Contract Upholstery, Church Flooring
